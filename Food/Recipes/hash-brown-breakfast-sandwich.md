# Hash Brown Breakfast Sandwich
![](https://www.eatingwell.com/thmb/cNh59yfRA4EeMIhUB7XV3bXV4MI=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc()/EWL-HashbrownEggAndCheeseSandwich-196_preview_maxWidth_4000_maxHeight_4000_ppi_300_quality_100-b3eb660b99114a3abd755800871e8e77.jpg)

> This quick breakfast sandwich swaps bread for crispy hash brown patties for a fun, satisfying twist and is ready in ten minutes.

> Source: https://www.eatingwell.com/hash-brown-egg-cheese-sandwich-11973932

## Ingredients

- 2 frozen hash brown patties
- 1 slice Cheddar or American cheese
- 1 leaf lettuce
- 1 slice tomato
- 1 large egg, fried
- 2 teaspoons ketchup

## Steps

1. Cook 2 hash brown patties according to package directions.
2. Top 1 hash brown with 1 cheese slice, 1 lettuce leaf, 1 tomato slice and the fried egg; drizzle with 2 teaspoons ketchup and top with the remaining hash brown.
