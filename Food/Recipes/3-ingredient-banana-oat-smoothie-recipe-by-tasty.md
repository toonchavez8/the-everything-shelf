# 3-Ingredient Banana Oat Smoothie Recipe by Tasty
![](https://img.buzzfeed.com/thumbnailer-prod-us-east-1/5eeb78d0e7af4e59867fa6c19ba01825/BFV32433_3-IngredientBreakfasts-FB1080.jpg?resize=1200:*)

> This 3-ingredient banana oat smoothie recipe is a quick and easy way to enjoy a healthy breakfast or snack. With just bananas, oats, and almond milk, this smoothie is both simple and filling.

> Source: https://tasty.co/recipe/3-ingredient-banana-oat-smoothie

## Video

https://vid.tasty.co/output/61659/mp4_640x640/1510600164

## Ingredients

- ½ cup rolled oats
- 1 banana
- 1 cup milk, of choice

## Steps

1. Add rolled oats to a blender and blend until the oats are the size of a fine crumb.
2. Add the banana and milk and blend well.
3. Pour in a glass.
4. Enjoy!
