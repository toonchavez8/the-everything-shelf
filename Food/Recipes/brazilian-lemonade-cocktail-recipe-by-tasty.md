# Brazilian Lemonade Cocktail Recipe by Tasty
![](https://img.buzzfeed.com/thumbnailer-prod-us-east-1/video-api/assets/548754/brazlemcocktail.jpg?resize=1200:*)

> This Brazilian Lemonade Cocktail is a creamy, citrusy drink that’s a great way tway to get a party started. It comes together in a blender in under 10 minutes and tastes like a vacation in a glass. The secret is blending whole limes (pith removed) directly with water, which gives the drink a fresh, slightly bitter edge that balances beautifully with sweetened condensed milk. Add vodka or cachaça for a boozy twist on the classic Brazilian limonada suíça, a beloved street drink found throughout Brazil. It's perfect for warm-weather entertaining, backyard parties, or any time you want something cold, creamy, and refreshing.

## Ingredients

- 2 large limes, rinsed, ends trimmed, quartered, and central pith removed
- 1 ½ cups cold water
- 1 ½ cups ice
- 4 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
- 4 fl oz vodka or cachaça
- 2 teaspoons sugar, optional, for additional sweetness
- lime, sliced into wheels, for garnish
- ice, for serving

## Steps

1. Add the quartered limes and cold water to a blender. Blend for 2 to 3 seconds to break down the limes without extracting too much bitterness.
2. Strain the lime mixture through a fine-mesh strainer, gently pressing the limes to extract their liquid.
3. Add the strained lime liquid back to the blender with the ice, sweetened condensed milk, and vodka or cachaça. Blend for about 15 seconds, until creamy and frothy.
4. Taste, and if desired, add 1 to 2 teaspoons of sugar for additional sweetness, then blend again until incorporated.
5. Fill glasses with ice, pour the cocktail directly over the ice, and garnish a lime wheel for each glass. Serve immediately.
6. Enjoy!
