# Sweet Potato Gingerbread Winter Smoothie Recipe by Tasty

## Ingredients

- ½ cup mashed sweet potato
- 1 tablespoon almond butter
- 1 tablespoon molasses
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 cup milk, of choice
- 1 pinch salt

## Steps

1. In a blender, combine the sweet potato, almond butter, molasses, vanilla, cinnamon, ginger, milk, and salt. Blend on high speed for 3 minutes, or until completely smooth.
2. Transfer the sweet potato mixture to a small saucepan and heat over medium-low heat until just warmed through. Do not boil.
3. Pour into a mug and serve immediately.
4. Enjoy!
