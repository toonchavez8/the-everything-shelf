# Sweet Potato Avocado Toast Recipe by Tasty
![](https://img.buzzfeed.com/thumbnailer-prod-us-east-1/3a6b86e7843544ab86bf1db2ab77c8eb/BFV20653_Easy3-CourseBrunch_FB-Upload.jpg?resize=1200:*)

> Sweet potato avocado toast is a delicious twist on the classic avocado toast. The sweetness of the potato pairs perfectly with the creaminess of the avocado, and the crunch of the toast makes for a satisfying bite. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, or a quick snack!

> Source: https://tasty.co/recipe/sweet-potato-avocado-toast

## Ingredients

- 1 large sweet potato
- 1 large avocado
- 2 garlics, minced
- ¾ cup cherry tomato
- bacon, crumbled, for garnish
- feta cheese, crumbled, for garnish

## Steps

1. Slice sweet potato lengthwise into ¼-inch (6 mm) thick slices.
2. Place slices in toaster on high for about 5 minutes, or until cooked through. (You may need to toast them several times depending on the strength your toaster settings)
3. While the sweet potato is toasting, mash the avocado in a bowl. Add garlic and tomatoes. Stir to combine.
4. Top your sweet potato toasts with the mashed avocado mixture and garnish with crumbled bacon and feta.
5. Serve immediately.
6. Enjoy!
