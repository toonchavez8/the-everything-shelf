# Strawberry Banana Raspberry Freezer-Prep Smoothie Recipe by Tasty
![](https://img.buzzfeed.com/thumbnailer-prod-us-east-1/b2bb1a7b70ed4b5e8b3a72ba7ba54357/BFV12235_Smoothies4Ways-FB1080SQ.jpg?resize=1200:*)

> Kick-start your day with a burst of fruity freshness with this delicious and nutritious smoothie. Packed with juicy strawberries, sweet bananas, and tangy raspberries, this smoothie is a perfect way to fuel your day.

> Source: https://tasty.co/recipe/strawberry-banana-raspberry-freezer-prep-smoothie

## Ingredients

- 1 banana, sliced
- 1 cup strawberry
- 1 cup rasberries
- 2 cups milk

## Steps

1. Mix fruits together in a plastic bag.
2. Seal bag and place in freezer for up to 6 months.
3. When ready, mix milk and fruit in a blender until consistency is smooth
4. Enjoy!
