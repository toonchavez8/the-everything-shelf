# Key Lime Pie Ice Cream Sandwich
![](https://hips.hearstapps.com/hmg-prod/images/aaa769d2-4935-4706-8cd4-be5678a9db8d.jpg?crop=0.5xw:1xh;0.462xw,0xh&resize=1200:*)

> These key lime pie ice cream sandwiches have the same tart and creamy vibes of the classic dessert, but in a handheld version perfect for too-hot summer days.

> Source: https://www.delish.com/cooking/recipe-ideas/a72793199/key-lime-pie-ice-cream-sandwich-recipe/

## Ingredients

- 1 (8-oz.) pkg. cream cheese, softened
- 2 tsp. finely grated Key lime zest
- 1/2 c. fresh Key lime juice
- 2 c. heavy cream
- 1 (14-oz.) can sweetened condensed milk
- 18 graham crackers

## Steps

1. Line a 13" x 9" dish with parchment, leaving a 2" overhang on all sides. In a large bowl, using a handheld mixer on medium-high speed, beat cream cheese, lime zest, and lime juice until smooth. Add cream and beat until soft peaks form. Add milk and continue to beat until stiff peaks form.
2. Line bottom of prepared dish with graham crackers, breaking to fit as needed. Top with whipped cream mixture and spread in an even layer. Top with remaining graham crackers, breaking to fit as needed.
3. Freeze until solid, at least 6 hours and up to 2 weeks.
4. When ready to serve, using parchment overhang, lift out sandwich and transfer to a cutting board. Cut into squares.
