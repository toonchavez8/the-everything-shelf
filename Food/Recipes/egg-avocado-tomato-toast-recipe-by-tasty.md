# Egg, Avocado, & Tomato Toast Recipe by Tasty
![](https://img.buzzfeed.com/thumbnailer-prod-us-east-1/fdefc6a60e104230863602130889a0f8/BFV29670_4BreakfastsUnderTenMinutes.jpg?resize=1200:*)

> The Egg Avocado Tomato Toast is a delicious and healthy breakfast option that is perfect for a busy morning.

> Source: https://tasty.co/recipe/egg-avocado-tomato-toast

## Video

https://vid.tasty.co/output/57653/mp4_640x640/1508540059

## Ingredients

- 1 slice bread
- 1 avocado
- 2 cherry tomatoes, sliced
- 1 egg

## Steps

1. Sliced two cherry tomatoes.
2. Mash ½ of 1 avocado with salt. (Use the other half for another toast.)
3. Prepare bread in a toaster to desired crispiness.
4. In a pan, fry an egg sunny side up.
5. Assemble the toast by spreading avocado on the toast, then the tomatoes and topping everything with the egg.
6. Enjoy!
