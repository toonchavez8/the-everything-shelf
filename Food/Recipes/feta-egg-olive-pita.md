# Feta, Egg & Olive Pita
![](https://www.eatingwell.com/thmb/rTbDhooOpEQbCu2wif8lXOicvMY=/1500x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc()/EWL-FetaEggAndOlivePita-238_preview_maxWidth_4000_maxHeight_4000_ppi_300_quality_100-e9b7f83479f9484ea3eeb73a9b56d311.jpg)

> This quick pita sandwich brings together bold flavors in just minutes. Creamy yogurt, briny olives and feta pair perfectly with a jammy egg and fresh vegetables.

> Source: https://www.eatingwell.com/feta-egg-olive-pita-11973891

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons whole-milk plain strained yogurt, such as Greek-style
- ½ whole-wheat pita
- ¼ cup baby arugula
- 2 tablespoons halved cherry tomatoes
- 1 large egg, poached
- 3 pitted Kalamata olives, chopped
- 1 tablespoon crumbled feta cheese
- ¼ teaspoon za’atar

## Steps

1. Spread 2 tablespoons yogurt inside pita half. Fill the pita with ¼ cup arugula, 2 tablespoons tomatoes, the poached egg, the chopped olives and 1 tablespoon feta. Sprinkle with ¼ teaspoon za’atar.
