# Ensalada De Coditos Con Jamón (Macaroni & Ham Salad)
![](https://cdn.apartmenttherapy.info/image/upload/f_jpg,q_auto:eco,c_fill,g_auto,w_1500,ar_16:9/tk%2Fphoto%2F2025%2F04-2025%2F2025-04-ensalada-de-coditos-con-jamon%2Fensalada-de-coditos-con-jamon-054)

> Creamy, spicy and slightly tangy, you&#x27;ll love the surprise pop of salty ham and cheesy goodness.

> Source: https://www.thekitchn.com/ensalada-de-coditos-con-jamon-recipe-23724985

## Ingredients

- 1 pound dried macaroni, fusilli, or cavatappi pasta
- 1 1/2 cups small diced cooked ham (about 7 1/2 ounces)
- 1 (about 15-ounce) can corn kernels, rinsed and drained
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup small-diced Monterey Jack cheese (optional, about 4 1/2 ounces)
- 1 medium red bell pepper, small-diced (about 1 cup)
- 1 medium green bell pepper, small-diced (about 1 cup)
- 2 medium stalks celery, small-diced (about 3/4 cup)
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1/4 cup finely chopped pickled jalapeños
- 1 tablespoon pickled jalapeño brine (optional)
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more as needed
- 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, plus more as needed
- Tostadas, for serving
- Hot sauce, for serving

## Steps

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over medium-high heat. Add 1 pound dried macaroni and cook according to package directions until al dente. Drain and spread onto a baking sheet to cool for at least 15 minutes.
2. Meanwhile, stir 1 1/2 cups small-diced cooked ham, 1 (about 15-ounce) can drained and rinsed corn kernels, 1 cup mayonnaise, 1 cup small-diced Monterey Jack cheese if using, 1 small-diced medium red bell pepper, 1 small-diced medium green bell pepper, 2 small-diced medium celery stalks, 1/2 cup sour cream, 1/4 cup finely chopped pickled jalapeños, 1 tablespoon pickled jalapeño brine if using, 1 teaspoon kosher salt, and 1/2 teaspoon black pepper together in a large bowl.
3. Stir in the pasta until evenly combined. Taste and season with more kosher salt and black pepper as needed.
4. Refrigerate for at least 20 minutes and up to 24 hours before serving. Taste and season with more kosher salt as needed. If pasta salad is dry, stir in up to 1/4 cup more mayonnaise or sour cream. Serve with tostadas and hot sauce.
5. — Recipe Notes —
6. Storage: Refrigerate in an airtight container for up to 4 days.
