# Banana Matcha Smoothie Recipe by Tasty
![](https://img.buzzfeed.com/thumbnailer-prod-us-east-1/2801d5e5f28b4ddd88c68c11cabdf3b5/FR_FB_CaffeineSmootheis.jpg?resize=1200:*)

> Whip up this energizing Banana Matcha smoothie for a refreshing and nutritious start to your day. With the perfect blend of fruity sweetness and a hint of earthy matcha, this smoothie will have you feeling like a morning superstar!

> Source: https://tasty.co/recipe/banana-matcha

## Video

https://vid.tasty.co/output/99086/landscape_480/1529361006

## Ingredients

- 1 banana, sliced
- 1 tablespoon matcha powder, plus more for serving
- 1 cup almond milk
- 1 cup fresh spinach
- 2 cups ice

## Steps

1. Add the banana, matcha, almond milk, spinach, and ice to a blender.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Serve in a glass topped with a sprinkle of matcha.
4. Enjoy!
