# Kiwi Apple Banana Freezer-Prep Smoothie Recipe by Tasty
![](https://img.buzzfeed.com/thumbnailer-prod-us-east-1/75c126d5e0624f04917490bf190bc3ef/BFV12235_Smoothies4Ways-FB1080SQ.jpg?resize=1200:*)

> Take a tropical escape with this refreshing smoothie that's loaded with fresh kiwi, crisp apple, and sweet banana. It's the perfect drink to cool off on a hot day or to start your day off on the right foot.

> Source: https://tasty.co/recipe/kiwi-apple-banana-freezer-prep-smoothie

## Ingredients

- 1 cup kiwi, sliced
- 1 cup apple, sliced
- 1 banana, sliced
- 2 cups milk

## Steps

1. Mix fruits together in a plastic bag.
2. Seal bag and place in freezer for up to 6 months.
3. When ready, mix milk and fruit in a blender until consistency is smooth.
4. Enjoy!
