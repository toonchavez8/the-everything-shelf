# Strawberry Banana Smoothie Meal Prep Recipe by Tasty
![](https://img.buzzfeed.com/thumbnailer-prod-us-east-1/013e3dfd6a1949dc9ddcb2357ade5f97/BFV16378_BreakfastSmoothieMealPrep4Ways_FB1080SQ.jpg?resize=1200:*)

> This classic smoothie is a delicious way to start your day. Sweet strawberries and ripe bananas are blended together to create a creamy and satisfying drink that will keep you going all morning.

> Source: https://tasty.co/recipe/strawberry-banana-smoothie-meal-prep

## Video

https://vid.tasty.co/output/21229/mp4_640x640/1484865382

## Ingredients

- 2 cups strawberry, sliced
- 1 banana, sliced
- 1 ½ cups milk, of choice
- 1 cup plain greek yogurt

## Steps

1. Put fruit in a freezer bag. Seal and store in freezer for up to 8 -12 months.
2. When ready to use, put milk, Greek yogurt, and frozen fruit into a blender and mix until consistency is smooth.
3. Enjoy!
